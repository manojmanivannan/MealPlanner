The "Base + Add-on" Model

Cook one shared vegetarian base for meals. The vegetarian adds plant-based protein (paneer, tofu) while the omnivore adds their choice of meat or fish. This saves time and unites your dining experience.

Time-Saving Prep

Dedicate a small amount of time on the weekend to batch cook bases, chop veggies, and marinate proteins. This makes weekday cooking a quick 30-45 minute assembly job.

Mindful Portion Control

Use your hand as a simple guide to portion sizes. Protein should be palm-sized, fats thumb-sized, and carbs a cupped hand. Fill half your plate with non-starchy vegetables.
